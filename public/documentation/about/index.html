Your Premier Staking & Presale Launchpad Platform

BNB DOG INU is a cutting-edge platform designed to empower users with tools for decentralized finance. We offer robust and secure services for both staking and token presales, simplifying the process for creators and participants alike.

1. Our Services

We provide two core services to support the growth of new and existing token projects on the BNB Chain:

  • Staking as a Service: Our platform allows users to effortlessly create and manage their own staking pools. This service enables project teams to reward their token holders and build a strong, engaged community. **Staking rewards must be provided to ensure a rewarding experience for all participants.** A pool creator can close their pool if there are no stakers or if all stakers have unstaked their tokens.
  • Presale Launchpad as a Service: We offer a user-friendly launchpad for new projects to conduct fair and transparent presales. Our platform automates the presale process, providing a secure environment for fundraising.
  • Security & Integrity: To protect our community, we reserve the right to block any presale if the associated token contract is found to contain malicious code. This is a crucial step to ensure a safe and secure environment for all participants.

2. Platform Fees

To sustain and improve our platform, a nominal fee is charged for the creation of new pools and presales. These fees help us maintain our infrastructure, enhance security, and develop new features.

  • Staking Pool Creation: 0.1 BNB per pool
  • New Presale Creation: 0.1 BNB per presale
  • Amount Raised in Presale: 1% of the total amount

3. Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation within the decentralized finance ecosystem by providing reliable, secure, and accessible tools for everyone. We believe in transparency and empowering our community to take part in the future of blockchain technology.

4. Contact

For any inquiries or support, please feel free to reach out to us. We are dedicated to helping our community succeed.
